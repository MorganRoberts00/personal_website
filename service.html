Service

My hometown of Longview does a yearly canned food drive to provide a thanksgiving dinner for those in need. Typically, the volunteers meet at Maude Cobb, the local convention center, where we spend the day unpacking and sorting canned food that has been graciously donated for the occasion. On top of being for a good cause, its also fun to hang out with people in the community.

Once a year, the Longview Library is transformed into a haunted house. The volunteers are in charge of dressing up the interior as well as participating in scaring the people who navigate the haunted house. It's a fun experience that teaches teamwork and gives back to the community.
